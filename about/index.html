About

My research field is high energy physics. My research interests are (1) Nature of the Higgs Boson, and related model building, collider phenomenology; (2) Nature of Dark Matter, and searches for various dark matter candidates; (3) Early Universe: Baryon Asymmetry, Inflation, and large scale structure.
